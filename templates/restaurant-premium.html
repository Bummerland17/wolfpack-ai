The Kitchen

Cooking that comes from somewhere real.

Chef Marcus Webb grew up in eastern Tennessee with a grandmother who kept a garden and a cast-iron skillet that never fully cooled. Fifteen years in Michelin-starred kitchens taught him technique. Coming home taught him the rest.

At Ember & Ash, the fire is both method and philosophy. We cook over actual flames, with actual farmers, in a city that actually cares about its food.

Marcus Webb

Executive Chef & Co-Owner
